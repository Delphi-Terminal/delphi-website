Data

We aggregate real-time and historical market data from 10+ prediction market exchanges, including Kalshi, Polymarket, and many more, into a single unified API. Markets are matched and standardized across exchanges so you get the full picture, not just one exchange's view.

Explore the API

Indices

We build cross-exchange indices that benchmark elections, macro outcomes, and geopolitical risks. Think of it as an S&P 500 for real-world events, built on data from multiple exchanges so no single platform can skew the signal.

News

Delphi News delivers unbiased, non-partisan reporting backed by prediction market data. Instead of editorial opinion, the wisdom of the crowd guides the story, separating signal from noise.
