Frequently Asked Questions

What is Delphi Terminal?

Delphi Terminal is an aggregated prediction market data terminal that provides institutional-grade L1, L2, and L3 market data across multiple exchanges including Kalshi, Polymarket, Limitless, and Opinion Labs. It offers both a web-based terminal interface and a comprehensive API for accessing real-time and historical prediction market data.

What prediction market exchanges does Delphi support?

Delphi aggregates data from 7+ prediction market exchanges including Kalshi, Polymarket, PredictIt, Limitless, Opinion Labs, Metaculus, and Manifold. We continuously add new exchanges to provide the most comprehensive market coverage available.

What is L1, L2, and L3 market data?

L1 (Level 1) data includes top-of-book prices and basic market information. L2 (Level 2) data provides full order book depth, showing all bids and asks at every price level. L3 (Level 3) data is the most granular — full order-by-order data showing every individual order placed, modified, or cancelled in the market.

Does Delphi offer an API?

Yes. Delphi provides a comprehensive REST and WebSocket API for accessing real-time and historical prediction market data. The API supports L1, L2, and L3 data feeds across all supported exchanges. Full documentation is available at api.delphiterminal.co/docs.

Who is Delphi built for?

Delphi is built for traders, quantitative researchers, market makers, hedge funds, academic researchers, and anyone who needs comprehensive prediction market data. Whether you're building trading algorithms, conducting research, or analyzing market sentiment, Delphi provides the data infrastructure you need.

How does Delphi's market matching work?

Delphi's proprietary market matching engine identifies and links equivalent or related prediction markets across different exchanges, enabling cross-exchange comparison and analysis. This allows users to see how the same event is priced across Kalshi, Polymarket, and other platforms in a single unified view.
