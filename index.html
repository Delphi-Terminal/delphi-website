Who We Are

Mission

We believe prediction markets deserve Wall-Street-grade infrastructure. Delphi builds the fastest, most reliable data and analytics engine in the space — so traders, researchers, and institutions can see the future more clearly.

How We Build

We design Delphi like a trading system, not a toy app. We optimize for speed, reliability, and clarity: real-time pipelines, fully normalized schemas, and analytics that stay close to the metal. Every feature starts from a simple question — would a serious trader or desk actually depend on this? If the answer's no, we don't ship it.

Why We're Different

We built Delphi from a trader's perspective — we've lived the microstructure, the bottlenecks, and the speed requirements. Our system doesn't just stream data; it understands it. We normalize every venue, link related markets, and surface cross-market signals others miss. That's why institutional traders trust Delphi for clean, consistent, context-rich data that meets true financial-market standards.

What we do

Delphi centralizes fragmented prediction markets into a unified platform, enabling traders and institutions to analyze data in one place. We eliminate the need for custom infrastructure setups and data pipelines, saving traders 40+ hours of initial setup and 8+ hours of weekly maintenance.

Real-Time Data Aggregation

Clean and aggregate data from 10+ prediction market exchanges in real time. Our system processes live websocket streams and exchange APIs, ensuring you always have the latest market data at your fingertips.

Normalized Data Structure

Transform unnormalized, fragmented exchange data into a unified structure. Every venue is standardized, related markets are linked, and cross-market signals are surfaced automatically.

Query Language & Analytics

Powerful querying language for price and orderbook data enables systematic research across thousands of markets. Build fast with an easily queryable interface designed for institutional trading.

Custom Functions & Tools

Advanced analytics including volatility surfaces, scenario analyses, and market-specific functions. Features that traders typically spend months manually developing are built-in and ready to use.

Seamless Integrations

Facilitate integrations with existing trading systems and models. Our API and Excel plug-in make it easy to incorporate Delphi data into your current workflow without disruption.

Institutional-Grade Infrastructure

Built for reliability and scale. Unlike retail-focused solutions, Delphi provides the uptime, data quality, and performance standards that professional traders and institutions require.
